# Kid Taco Meat

## Ingredients
1. Baby Goat (Kid)
2. Seasonings or whatever

## What to do
1. Punch baby goat real good until it's tender.
2. Butcher
3. Season.
4. Cook it up.
5. Put it in your taco.
